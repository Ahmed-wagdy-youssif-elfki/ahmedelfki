Our Mission

Equipping American athletes and their loyal companions with unparalleled performance gear.

Born from Passion.

What started as a small garage project in Austin, Texas, has evolved into a premier online destination for active individuals and pet lovers across the United States. We realized that those who push themselves in the gym often share their adventures with their dogs, yet no single retailer catered to both with high-end, durable quality.

At ActivePaws, we meticulously source our materials to ensure our compression wear withstands grueling CrossFit sessions, while our pet accessories are tough enough for the most energetic trail runs. We cut out the middlemen to bring you direct-to-consumer premium goods.
